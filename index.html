Empowering fairness, dignity and clarity.

This platform exists to restore fairness in a system where fear, confusion and imbalance too often rule.

Welcome to Your Debt Support Hub. Over the past five years, personal debt in the UK has risen sharply — not because people were reckless, but because many were caught in circumstances beyond their control.

What Happened?

  • Lockdown and Furlough (2020): Millions were placed on furlough at 80% salary. Spending dropped, but bills did not.
  • Energy Crisis (2021–2022): Supplier collapses and soaring tariffs trapped households, especially those unable to switch providers.
  • Cost-of-Living Pressures: Inflation surged, wages stagnated, and essentials became more expensive — forcing many to rely on credit.
  • Financial Institutions’ Role: Borrowing was pushed heavily post-lockdown. When repayments faltered, debts were sold to agencies prioritising profit, not fairness.

The Reality in Numbers

117% Debt-to-Income

By mid-2025, households owed more than their yearly disposable income.

Rising Consumer Credit

Borrowing rebounded by 2024, driven by credit cards and personal loans.

Energy Arrears Surge

Millions fell behind on bills during the 2022 energy crisis with no escape route.

30,494 Insolvencies

Q2 2025 insolvencies highlight systemic failure — not personal failure.

Sources: Bank of England, House of Commons Library, Citizens Advice

Our Promise

This site offers free, unbiased, and non-judgmental advice. We understand debt isn’t about luxury — it’s about survival in a system that failed many. Our mission is to help you understand your rights, challenge unfair practices, and regain control with clarity and confidence.

Why This Matters

A victim is a victim — financial, legal, medical or emotional. Justice should never depend on a person's bank balance, especially when hardship was caused by events outside their control.

We are not solicitors, nor do we claim to be. We aim to create awareness and self-empowerment so you can take back control with confidence and dignity.
